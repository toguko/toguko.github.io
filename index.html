12 facts
about me
1. More than 10 years in Interaction Design
I specialize in UX/UI Design and Interaction Design. I have more than a decade experience in these fields. My passion is creating beautiful and easy to use digital products.
2. Designing complex products
I have got an experience designing (UX & UI) massive complex projects like social nets, CRM, messengers, etc. I like to solve the problems associated with large business systems.
3. Work on services with 40 million users per day
I developed a multiplatform design (web and mobile) for YandexNews, the biggest news service in the Eastern Europe.
4. Integrating designers and developers flows
During my work at Avito, I created workflow allowing for contracting a gap between designer and developers. These solutions were based on a unified UIKit and several tools helping to translate design to an implementation. Now some of these tools are open source projects ().
5. Designing for multiplatform
I've developed a design for different platforms (tv, web, mobile, wearable). I understand their restrictions and opportunities well, and I can find more scalable decisions for a solving of design tasks.
6. Having an experience with Augmented Reality
I love technology and love to experiment with one. One of my recent projects is about augmented reality.
7. I can code
I know HTML (Pug), CSS (Stylus), Javascript (CoffeScript) and Swift. It is very useful knowledge to me as a designer. On the first side, it helps me to understand technical restriction of my design, on the other hand, it allows creating complex live prototypes and verifying my design concepts. There is an interview with me about this stuff and cool tutorial about my design concept.
8. Creating hi-fidelity interactive prototypes
If I need to check my design decisions, I create a prototype for this. I usually use FramerJS for multiplatform decisions. Also sometimes I build a native prototype for iOS.
9. Always improve my design skills
I try to create different design concepts each day. It could be anything from new cool UI interaction to a product idea. A lot of them I post on Dribbble. By the way, some of my concepts are featured by different design magazines.
10. Developed a lot of design tools
The Sketch is my favorite tool for designing. I always try to automize my work routine. So I am creating different plugins helping me to do that. And more of them are open source projects.
11. I love art
I liked to learn a history of world art when I was a student (I am an anthropologist by an education). I understand a difference between impressionism and expressionism. Though, personally, I prefer suprematism and abstract expressionism.
12. Playing the guitar
Music is my the second passion after design. I love to experiment with sounds. Here you can check it out.
